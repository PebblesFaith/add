How It Works



Step 1

If you have a valid state identification card such as, a driver license, state, or a real ID driver license card than you should receive an official registration alphanumeric card number from your state Department of Motor Vehicle through the United States Postal Service Delivery.

Step 2

Next, register your state sent Department of Motor Vehicle registration alphanumeric card number by way of the iVoteBallot.com website in order to sign up for your state online election account.

Step 3

And, if you have entered your state's Department of Motor Vehicle registration alphanumeric and identification cards numbers successfully which matches to your state database details than you are allowed to officially vote for each of your state and presidential election online.
